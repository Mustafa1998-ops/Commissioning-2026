# Daily Reflection

Today marked a significant milestone as I practically engaged with essential tools for my professional development and the Grey Sentinel 45-day program. Almost everything I tackled today involved completely new practical applications. Furthermore, the practical application of virtualization through VMware was entirely new. While the concept of virtual machines is fundamental in IT, actually deploying a hypervisor, configuring the sandboxed environment, and managing the isolated operating system firsthand was a novel and eye-opening experience.
